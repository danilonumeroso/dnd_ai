## Turn 2

### Dungeon Master

Aerion peers into the shifting grey veil, but the damp cold seems to numb his senses, making every shadow look like a phantom. Before the party can react, three small, jagged-toothed figures leap from the dense thicket, their rusted scimitars raised high.

Three Goblins ambush the party from the thick fog!
